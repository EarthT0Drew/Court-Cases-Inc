Overview

      Ellsberg released the papers to the New York Times, but the government (Nixion Administration) obtained a court order to prevent the publishing. Therefore, Ellsberg released them to Washington Post, and the government tried to do the same thing, but the court refused. After the government appealed the case, the Supreme court ruled, 6-3, that the prior restraint as unconstitutional.
