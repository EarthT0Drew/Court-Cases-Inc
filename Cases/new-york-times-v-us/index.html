New York Times vs. USA

Quick Info

Petitioner:
New York Times Company
Respondent:
United States of America

Constitutional question:
Did the Nixion Administration violate the Freedom of Press?

Decision:
Yes, the Nixion Administration did violate the Freedom of Press.
Vote:
6-3

Date argued:
June 26th, 1971
Date decided:
June 30th, 1971
